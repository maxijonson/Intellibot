***Joke***
Everyone needs a little laugh once in a while! That's why I've been given the gift of humor!

***Warning***
I come with a set of inappropriate jokes, such as dirty jokes and dark, very dark humor. Don't worry, unless you explicitly authorize me to tell you one of those, I won't tell you those. However, if you do authorize me, you have been warned, I am not responsible if you are offended. I just took them off Google, after all.

***Joke Number***
Just ignore these, they're mostly for my owner to track which joke it is in case he wants to delete it!

***Syntax***
__*Tell random filtered joke*__
```${prefix}joke```
__*Tell random joke (unfiltered)*__
```${prefix}joke nofilter```
__*Tell joke from a category*__
```${prefix}joke <category>```
__*View categories*__
```${prefix}joke categories```
